Slack workspace and Trello board

2021-02-20 1 min read

Our DS Squad Slack workspace is our primary communication platform. Use our Slack workspace to send messages to the group, @mention team members, send direct messages, and share files.

Trello is our Kanban-style tool for organizing and displaying the tasks we are working on, tasks that need to get done, and which team members are working on them. Use our Trello Task Tracker Board to help the team stay informed and monitor our progress.
